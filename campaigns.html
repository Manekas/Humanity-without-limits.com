Help Palestine!

"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give - " Winston Churchill

Due to the ongoing war between Iran and Palestine has lead to a lot of destruction to property and intellect of people. we as a an organisation aim at helping the people affected by this turmoil. By the money provided we aim at making resources like food,water,living space and many more for the affected. 

                                    
Health and Wellness Initiatives!

"Wellness is a journey,not a destination. Enjoy each step -" Anne Frank

Our health and wellness initiatives aim at teaching people the journey to a healthy and peaceful mental,physical and social life. The programme itself is very interactive with people. it involves people participating in various modules which each contain 3-4 activities and each activity aiming at a major and important componet of a healthy lifestyle. 

                                    
Education Support Programmes!

"Fundraising is the gentle art of teaching the joy of giving - " Hank Rosso

Education is the greatest gift a person can give to another person. We as an organisation aim at providing education to the group of people that are below the poverty line and cant afford to educate their children. Education is the key aspect towards eradicating major social evils like poverty, corruption and many other. The funds aquired by our organisation is allocated towards building schools and finding teachers for this purpose. 

                                    
Cultural Preservation Projects!

"A single act of kindness is like a drop of oil on a patch of dry skin -- seeping,spreading and affecting more than the original need-" Richelle E.Goodrich

With the increase in digitalisation with every passing day importance of culture in our society is decreasing with every new day. Due to this certain cultures are on the verge of extinction and being completely wiped off the face of Earth. Humanity without Limits arranges certain programmes to let people know about such cultures and empower them to help us in making more people aware of these cultures.
